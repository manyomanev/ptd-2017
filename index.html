The Event

What:

Plovdiv Throwdown is a two-day competition taking place in the beautiful city of Plovdiv. The event is powered by MoVe Team and is taking place for a first year. 96 athletes will earn their spot in the finals, through the online qualifiers. First 32 men from the qualifiers will advance to the finals in RX division, 32 in division Men scaled, 16 women in RX division and 16 in Women scaled division Join us for an event full of spectacular challenges and remarkable athletes that you will not forget! It's going to be an amazing event to compete in or just to attend.

Where: Rowing Canal, Plovdiv, Bulgaria
